About Us

We provide capital for public companies with PIPE financing, stock loans, and non-toxic Equity Lines of Credit. We also purchase aged debt (2 years or older) from public companies and non-affiliates and we pay dollar for dollar. In addition, we make a direct investment into the public company equal to the amount of debt we purchase. We also provide Asset-Based financing secured with equipment, accounts receivable, and other liquid assets.

We prepare all Private Placement documents to allow companies to legally raise capital by complying with all State and Federal security laws. In addition, we arrange presentations with accredited investors qualified to make an investment.

We also take companies public with a reverse merger, S-1 Registration, or Form 10 filing. We handle all of the legal work, filings, arrange for market makers, access to capital, institutional and retail support for the stock.
