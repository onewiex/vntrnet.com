Capital. Liquidity. Exposure to the investment community.

VentureNet Capital Group, Inc. (stock symbol VNTN) provides capital for public companies from $1 million to $100 million dollars for companies listed on AMEX, NASDAQ, and OTC Bulletin Board. The Company also specializes in arranging introductions to investment banks, market makers, analysts, institutional investors, and arranging roadshows, investment conferences to enhance shareholder value and provide additional liquidity for their stock. VentureNet Capital Group, Inc. is a Founding Member of the Southern California Investment Association (SCIA), a 200 members-only association of investment bankers, private equity, hedge funds, and accredited investors who have funded millions of dollars in transactions every year since its Founding in 2000.

Capital for Public Companies
VentureNet Capital Group, Inc. also serves as an advisor to public companies to assist in capital formation strategies and investor relations consulting. The Company is very active in PIPE financing, stock loans, and non-toxic Equity Lines of Credit. VentureNet Capital also purchases aged debt from public companies and pays dollar for dollar for this debt. In addition, we make a direct investment in the public company equal to the amount of debt we purchase.

There are 15,000 public companies listed in the U.S. All are seeking access to capital and stock liquidity to build shareholder value and execute on their business models. VentureNet Capital Group, Inc. and its affiliate companies provide capital as well as enhance stock market values and liquidity for emerging public companies.
